About

I'm a holistic coach experienced in developing tailored training and nutrition solutions to help you achieve a thriving healthy lifestyle.

For the past 20 years  I’ve been an aerialist, a yoga teacher, a handstands practitioner, a gym rat, a vegetarian, a vegan (very very briefly) and a protein junkie.

Through my own trial and error and also the experiences I collected from my students and clients I learned to combine the best of the many disciplines, training methods, diets and habits to optimise a healthy lifestyle that fits you and your unique routine.

I specialize in:

  • Strength and resistance training
  • Mobility and flexibility
  • Nutrition Coaching
  • Habit training

I work side by side with my clients making every decision together and learning to pivot and adapt as we progress towards your goals.
